**Journal:** ACS Nano, 9(6), 5937–5946 **(Impact Factor: 13.9)**

Development of a bioresorbable electronic stent integrated with therapeutic nanoparticles for treating endovascular diseases. This work bridges nanomaterial engineering and biomedical device design, demonstrating a fully degradable smart stent platform with drug delivery capability.
