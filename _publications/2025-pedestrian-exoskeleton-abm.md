**Status:** In Preparation

**Authors:** Sanghun Shin (First author), Junseok Hwang

**Target Journal:** Gait & Posture

Agent-based modeling of mixed pedestrian populations using the **Social Force Model** to simulate interactions between typical pedestrians and exoskeleton users at urban crosswalks. Safety metrics analyzed include Time-to-Collision (TTC) and Post-Encroachment Time (PET).
