**Authors:** Selinsky, R. S., Shin, S., Lukowski, M. A., & Jin, S.

**Journal:** Journal of Physical Chemistry Letters, 3, 1649–1656 **(Impact Factor: 9.4)**

Synthesis of epitaxial heterostructures combining lead selenide (PbSe) quantum dots with hematite (α-Fe₂O₃) nanowires for photovoltaic applications. This work resolved a 3-year persistent research challenge at the NSF-funded Materials Chemistry Lab at UW-Madison.
